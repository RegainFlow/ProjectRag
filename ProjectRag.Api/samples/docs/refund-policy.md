# Refund Policy

Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of the original purchase date.

Refunds are allowed for duplicate payments, accidental overpayments, cancelled services that were not delivered, or billing errors confirmed by finance.

Refunds are not issued for completed professional services, custom development work, or usage-based charges that have already been consumed.

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method when possible. Processing usually takes five to ten business days after approval.

Refunds greater than 5,000 dollars require approval from the finance director.
