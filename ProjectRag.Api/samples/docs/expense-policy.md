# Expense Policy

Employees must submit expenses within 30 days of the transaction date.

Receipts are required for expenses over 25 dollars. Each expense report must include the business purpose, date, amount, vendor, and project or department code.

Air travel should be booked in economy class unless an executive approves an exception. Hotel stays should use preferred vendors when available.

Meals are reimbursable when travel or approved business meetings require them. Alcohol is not reimbursable unless explicitly approved by a department leader.

Expense reports are reviewed by the employee's manager and then processed by finance.
