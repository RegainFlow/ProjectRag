# Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely when their role, manager, and team commitments allow it.

Remote workers must maintain a reliable internet connection and a workspace suitable for confidential conversations.

Company data must be accessed through approved applications and secured devices. Public computers may not be used for company work.

Core collaboration hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in the employee's primary team time zone. Meetings outside those hours should be minimized.

Managers may require office attendance for planning sessions, customer meetings, onboarding, or performance concerns.
