# Invoice Disputes

Customers must submit invoice disputes within 15 calendar days of receiving the invoice.

A valid dispute must include the invoice number, disputed amount, reason for dispute, and supporting documentation. Common reasons include incorrect rates, missing credits, duplicate charges, or services not received.

Finance should acknowledge the dispute within two business days. While a dispute is under review, only the disputed portion is placed on hold. Undisputed amounts remain due under normal payment terms.

If the dispute is approved, finance issues a credit memo or corrected invoice. If the dispute is rejected, the customer must pay the outstanding balance within 10 days of the decision.
