# Data Retention Policy

Customer account records are retained for seven years after contract termination unless a legal hold requires longer retention.

Support tickets are retained for three years after closure. Product telemetry is retained for 18 months unless aggregated or anonymized.

Backups are retained for 35 days. Backup restoration requests must be approved by infrastructure leadership.

Personal data deletion requests must be reviewed by legal and privacy teams before processing.

Data under legal hold must not be deleted, altered, or anonymized until the legal hold is released.
